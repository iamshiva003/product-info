Additional Product Information

The Samsung Galaxy S20 is a smartphone that was released by Samsung in February 2020. It has a 6.2-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels, a 120Hz refresh rate, and HDR10+ support.

The S20 is powered by either a Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 or Samsung Exynos 990 processor, depending on the region. It has 8GB of RAM and comes with either 128GB or 256GB of internal storage, which can be expanded up to 1TB using a microSD card.

The phone has a triple rear camera system consisting of a 12-megapixel wide-angle lens, a 64-megapixel telephoto lens, and a 12-megapixel ultrawide lens. It can shoot 8K video at 24fps, 4K video at up to 60fps, and 1080p video at up to 240fps. There is also a 10-megapixel front-facing camera for selfies and video calls.

Other features of the Samsung S20 include 5G connectivity, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.0, NFC, and an in-display fingerprint sensor. It also has a 4,000mAh battery that supports 25W fast charging, 15W fast wireless charging, and 9W reverse wireless charging. The phone runs on Android 10 with Samsung's One UI 2.0 on top.
